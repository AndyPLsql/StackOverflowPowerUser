StackOverflow Power User
Stop wasting time while programming !

Options


If a question has a better answer than the accepted one, it shows an clickable arrow to easily notice it and avoid wasting time. (Try it)


If a question has no answer, it shows a disappointed smiley to easily notice it and avoid wasting time reading the question. (Try it)


Automatically scroll to the first answer, as you usually don't have to read the question. (Try it)


Shows a floating sidebar on the left that shows score of first 5 answers, as sometimes it can be interesting to read other well upvoted answers. It will also inform you if you have already upvoted an answer previously on this page. (Try it)


Give the ability to navigate through all answers without having to remove your hands from keyboard. (Try it)


You need to have at least 1000 reputation on StackOverflow for this feature to work. When arriving on Stack Overflow page, it automatically expand votes count (upvotes/downvote) and shows the score of the answer (in percent). (Try it)

NEW !
Just hide the new sidebar with "Home", "Tags", "Users", "Jobs", that may be useless for most users and takes too much space. (Try it)
